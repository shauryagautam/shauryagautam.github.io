Case Study 1: SaaS Platform User Onboarding Optimization

User Research A/B Testing Analytics Conversion Optimization

Problem Statement

A B2B SaaS platform was experiencing high drop-off rates during user onboarding, with only 45% of new signups completing the setup process. This resulted in low product activation and poor trial-to-paid conversion rates.

Discovery & Research

Conducted comprehensive user research to identify friction points:

  • Interviewed 25+ users who abandoned onboarding
  • Analyzed session recordings using Hotjar
  • Reviewed analytics data to identify drop-off points
  • Conducted competitive analysis of 5 similar products

Key Insights

  • Users found the initial setup too complex (7 steps)
  • Lack of clear value proposition during onboarding
  • No progress indicators causing uncertainty
  • Technical jargon confused non-technical users

Solution & Execution

Redesigned onboarding flow based on insights:

  • Reduced steps from 7 to 3 core actions
  • Added progress bar and estimated time
  • Implemented contextual tooltips and help
  • Created role-based onboarding paths
  • Added "skip for now" options for non-critical steps

Testing & Iteration

Ran A/B test with 50/50 traffic split over 4 weeks. Monitored completion rates, time-to-value, and user feedback. Made iterative improvements based on data.

Measurable Impact

  • 35% increase in onboarding completion rate (45% → 61%)
  • 40% reduction in time-to-value (12 min → 7 min)
  • 22% improvement in trial-to-paid conversion
  • 18% decrease in support tickets related to onboarding

Learnings

  • Simplicity wins - fewer steps led to higher completion
  • Progress indicators reduce anxiety and abandonment
  • Role-based personalization improves relevance
  • Continuous iteration based on data is crucial

Case Study 2: Mobile App Feature Prioritization Framework

RICE Framework Roadmapping Stakeholder Management Agile

Challenge

Product team had 50+ feature requests in backlog with no clear prioritization method. Stakeholders disagreed on priorities, leading to delayed decisions and team frustration.

Approach

Implemented RICE prioritization framework:

  • Reach: Number of users impacted per quarter
  • Impact: Effect on key metrics (0.25 to 3 scale)
  • Confidence: Data certainty (0% to 100%)
  • Effort: Person-months required

Implementation Process

  • Educated stakeholders on RICE methodology
  • Created scoring template in Notion
  • Facilitated workshops to score top 30 features
  • Built transparent roadmap based on RICE scores
  • Established quarterly review process

Results

  • 50% faster feature prioritization decisions
  • 85% stakeholder alignment on roadmap
  • 30% improvement in team velocity
  • Reduced feature scope creep by 40%

Case Study 3: E-commerce Checkout Flow Redesign

UX Research Conversion Optimization Usability Testing

Problem

E-commerce platform had 68% cart abandonment rate, significantly higher than industry average of 55%. This represented millions in lost revenue annually.

Research Methods

  • Conducted 15 moderated usability tests
  • Analyzed 200+ session recordings
  • Surveyed 500+ users who abandoned carts
  • Performed heuristic evaluation of checkout flow

Key Findings

  • Unexpected shipping costs at final step
  • Forced account creation before checkout
  • Complex form with 18 fields
  • Lack of trust signals (security badges)
  • No mobile optimization

Solution Implemented

  • Guest checkout option added
  • Shipping costs shown upfront
  • Reduced form fields to 8 essential ones
  • Added trust badges and secure payment icons
  • Optimized for mobile (60% of traffic)
  • Implemented progress indicator

Business Impact

  • 28% increase in conversion rate
  • 22% reduction in cart abandonment (68% → 53%)
  • $2.4M additional annual revenue
  • 35% faster checkout completion time
